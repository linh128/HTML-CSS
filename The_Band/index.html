chillies

We love music

Chillies is a Vietnamese pop/indie band formed in Ho Chi Minh City in 2018. Known for their warm melodies and heartfelt lyrics, they blend pop, alternative rock, and indie influences to create music that resonates with young audiences. Their songs often explore themes of love, hope, and self-discovery, offering both comfort and inspiration. With popular hits such as “Mascara,” “Và Thế Là Hết,” and “Cứ Chill Thôi,” Chillies has become one of the most beloved contemporary bands in Vietnam. Beyond streaming platforms, they actively perform at music festivals and live shows, where their natural stage presence and emotional connection with fans shine through. Chillies continues to grow as a symbol of modern Vietnamese music, bringing a fresh yet familiar sound that inspires listeners everywhere.
